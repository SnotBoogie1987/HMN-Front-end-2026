Scaling
Our Impact,
Transparently

In our first year trading as a full time agency, we turned over just shy of £250,000 worth of work with over 40 freelancers engaged.

All but two requests were fulfilled in 2024, both of which fell over the challenging Christmas period...

I mean, c'mon.

Whilst these numbers will be small fry to other, larger agencies, we are immensely proud of what we've already achieved.

We received (and were recently re-accredited) Living Wage Employer certification, validating our progressive understanding on rates and wages.

We are also a proud 1% For The Planet member, where 1% of our annual profits go straight to supporting other organisations helping people and the planet.

Our ambitions for the future are even bigger and bolder, read all about them below... we expect to be held accountable.

Client Welfare
Scoring

If our clients receive the best, it should be a two way street.

We know wrangling clients can be a tall order for production companies; plans often change on the ground without warning and hours can intensify, that is simply part and parcel of the film world.

That said, we're working with our clients to develop a simple framework that can help mitigate how these challenges can negatively impact freelancers when mismanaged.

This system will inherently provide a welfare-scoring system, providing experience-based transparency to freelancers without leaving our clients penalised.

Estimated implementation: Summer 2026

B Corporation
Certification

The B Corp certification isn't an end goal, it's our North Star guiding us to build a business that will always be a force for good.

Companies with 'B Corp' status have undergone rigorous, independent assessment from B Lab to ensure they meet high standards of social or environmental performance, transparency and accountability.

While this certification is rightfully seen as a mark of excellence, to us - it's the bare minimum.

Anticipated acquisition: September 2026
